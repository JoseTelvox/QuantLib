Detailed Description

Taiwanese calendars.

Holidays for the Taiwan stock exchange (data from https://www.twse.com.tw/en/trading/holiday.html):

  • Saturdays
  • Sundays
  • New Year's Day, January 1st
  • Peace Memorial Day, February 28
  • Labor Day, May 1st
  • Double Tenth National Day, October 10th

Other holidays for which no rule is given (data available for 2002-2024 only:)

  • Chinese Lunar New Year
  • Tomb Sweeping Day
  • Dragon Boat Festival
  • Moon Festival
