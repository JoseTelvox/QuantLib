\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Chilean calendars. 

Holidays for the Santiago \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_stock}{Stock} Exchange (data from \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Public_holidays_in_Chile}{\texttt{ https\+://en.\+wikipedia.\+org/wiki/\+Public\+\_\+holidays\+\_\+in\+\_\+\+Chile}})\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Saturdays 
\item Sundays 
\item New \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a5dcda5470aff130981ac30f25d4244e7}{Year}\textquotesingle{}s \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, January 1st 
\item January 2nd, when falling on a Monday (since 2017) 
\item Good Friday 
\item Easter Saturday 
\item Labour \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, May 1st 
\item Navy \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, May 21st 
\item \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day} of Aboriginal People, around June 21st (observed on each Winter Solstice) (since 2021) 
\item Saint Peter and Saint Paul, June 29th (moved to the nearest Monday if it falls on a weekday) 
\item Our Lady of Mount Carmel, July 16th 
\item Assumption \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, August 15th 
\item Independence \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, September 18th (also the 17th if the latter falls on a Monday or Friday) 
\item Army \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, September 19th (also the 20th if the latter falls on a Friday) 
\item Discovery of Two Worlds, October 12th (moved to the nearest Monday if it falls on a weekday) 
\item Reformation \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, October 31st (since 2008; moved to the preceding Friday if it falls on a Tuesday, or to the following Friday if it falls on a Wednesday) 
\item All Saints\textquotesingle{} \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, November 1st 
\item Immaculate Conception, December 8th 
\item Christmas \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, December 25th 
\item New \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a5dcda5470aff130981ac30f25d4244e7}{Year}\textquotesingle{}s Eve, December 31st; (see \href{https://www.cmfchile.cl/portal/prensa/615/w3-article-49984.html}{\texttt{ https\+://www.\+cmfchile.\+cl/portal/prensa/615/w3-\/article-\/49984.\+html}}) 
\end{DoxyItemize}
