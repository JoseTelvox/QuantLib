\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Gauss-\/\+Legendre (l,m,n)-\/p Scheme. 


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em l} & order of Gauss-\/\+Legendre integration within every fixed point iteration step \\
\hline
{\em m} & fixed point iteration steps, first step is a partial Jacobi-\/\+Newton, the rest are naive Richardson fixed point iterations \\
\hline
{\em n} & number of Chebyshev nodes to interpolate the exercise boundary \\
\hline
{\em p} & order of Gauss-\/\+Legendre integration in final conversion of the exercise boundary into option prices \\

\end{DoxyParams}
