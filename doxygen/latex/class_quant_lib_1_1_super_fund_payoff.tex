\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Binary supershare and superfund payoffs. 

Binary superfund payoff

Superfund sometimes also called "{}supershare"{}, which can lead to ambiguity; within \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib}{Quant\+Lib} the terms supershare and superfund are used consistently according to the definitions in Bloomberg OVX function\textquotesingle{}s help pages.

This payoff is equivalent to being (1/lowerstrike) a) long (short) an Asset\+Or\+Nothing Call (Put) at the lower strike and b) short (long) an Asset\+Or\+Nothing Call (Put) at the higher strike
