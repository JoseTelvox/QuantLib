\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
analytic Heston-\/model engine based on Fourier transform 

\doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_analytic_heston_engine_1_1_integration}{Integration} detail\+: Two algebraically equivalent formulations of the complex logarithm of the Heston model exist. Gatherals \mbox{[}2005\mbox{]} (also Duffie, Pan and \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_singleton}{Singleton} \mbox{[}2000\mbox{]}, and Schoutens, Simons and Tistaert\mbox{[}2004\mbox{]}) version does not cause discoutinuities whereas the original version (e.\+g. Heston \mbox{[}1993\mbox{]}) needs some sort of "{}branch correction"{} to work properly. Gatheral\textquotesingle{}s version does also work with adaptive integration routines and should be preferred over the original Heston version.
