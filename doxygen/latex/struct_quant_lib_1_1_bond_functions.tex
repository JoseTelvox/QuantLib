\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_bond}{Bond} adapters of \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_cash_flows}{Cash\+Flows} functions. 

See \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_cash_flows}{Cash\+Flows} for functions\textquotesingle{} documentation.

These adapters calls into \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_cash_flows}{Cash\+Flows} functions passing as input the \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_bond}{Bond} cashflows, the dirty price (i.\+e. npv) calculated from clean price, the bond settlement date (unless another date is given), zero ex-\/dividend days, and excluding any cashflow on the settlement date.

Prices are always clean, as per market convention.
