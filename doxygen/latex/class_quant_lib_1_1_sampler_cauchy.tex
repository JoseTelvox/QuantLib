Cauchy Sampler. 

\doxylink{struct_quant_lib_1_1_sample}{Sample} from cauchy distribution. This means that the parameter space must have support on the positive whole real line. For lower dimensions it could be faster than the Gaussian sampler, specially when combined with the Cauchy temperature.
