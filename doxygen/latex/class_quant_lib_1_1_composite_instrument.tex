\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Composite instrument 

This instrument is an aggregate of other instruments. Its NPV is the sum of the NPVs of its components, each possibly multiplied by a given factor.

\begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
Methods that drive the calculation directly (such as \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_lazy_object_a70c9b24e50132408d8d9ce4f6674662a}{recalculate()}, \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_lazy_object_a7157f41e83f2c5d924f5c5bafda04d1d}{freeze()} and others) might not work correctly. 
\end{DoxyWarning}
