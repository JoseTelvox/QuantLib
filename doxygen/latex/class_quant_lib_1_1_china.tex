\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Chinese calendar. 

Holidays\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Saturdays 
\item Sundays 
\item New \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a5dcda5470aff130981ac30f25d4244e7}{Year}\textquotesingle{}s day, January 1st (possibly followed by one or two more holidays) 
\item Labour \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, first week in May 
\item National \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, one week from October 1st 
\end{DoxyItemize}

Other holidays for which no rule is given (data available for 2004-\/2019 only)\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Chinese New \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a5dcda5470aff130981ac30f25d4244e7}{Year} 
\item Ching Ming Festival 
\item Tuen Ng Festival 
\item Mid-\/\+Autumn Festival 
\item 70th anniversary of the victory of anti-\/\+Japaneses war 
\end{DoxyItemize}
