\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Concrete date class. 

This class provides methods to inspect dates as well as methods and operators which implement a limited date algebra (increasing and decreasing dates, and calculating their difference).

\begin{DoxyRefDesc}{Test}
\item[\mbox{\hyperlink{test__test000178}{Test}}]self-\/consistency of dates, serial numbers, days of month, months, and weekdays is checked over the whole date range. \end{DoxyRefDesc}
