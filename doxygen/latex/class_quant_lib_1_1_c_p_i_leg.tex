\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Helper class building a sequence of capped/floored \doxylink{struct_quant_lib_1_1_c_p_i}{CPI} coupons. 

Also allowing for the inflated notional at the end... especially if there is only one date in the schedule. If the fixed rate is zero you get a \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_fixed_rate_coupon}{Fixed\+Rate\+Coupon}, otherwise you get a Zero\+Inflation\+Coupon.
