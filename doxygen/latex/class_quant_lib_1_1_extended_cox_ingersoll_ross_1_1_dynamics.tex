\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Short-\/rate dynamics in the extended Cox-\/\+Ingersoll-\/\+Ross model. 

The short-\/rate is here   \[    r(t) = \varphi(t) + y(t)
\] where $ \varphi(t) $ is the deterministic time-\/dependent parameter used for term-\/structure fitting and $ y_t $ is a standard CIR process with dynamics   \[    dy(t)=k(\theta-y(t))dt+\sigma \sqrt{y(t)}dW(t)
\]
