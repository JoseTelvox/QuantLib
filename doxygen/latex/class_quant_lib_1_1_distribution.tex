void Quant\+Lib\+::\+Distribution\+::tranche (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{namespace_quant_lib_a372ac5c1a422a6b276fe0552d4d83f50}{Real}}}]{attachment\+Point}{, }\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{namespace_quant_lib_a372ac5c1a422a6b276fe0552d4d83f50}{Real}}}]{detachment\+Point}{}\end{DoxyParamCaption})}

Transform the loss distribution into the tranche loss distribution for losses L\+\_\+T = min(\+L,\+D) -\/ min(\+L,\+A). The effects are\+: 1) shift the distribution to the left by A, then 2) cut off at D-\/A, Pr(L\+\_\+T \texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} D-\/A) = 0 3) ensure Pr(L\+\_\+T \texorpdfstring{$>$}{>}= 0) = 1, i.\+e. a density spike at L\+\_\+T = 0
