\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
overnight coupon 

Coupon paying the interest, depending on the averaging convention, due to daily overnight fixings.

\begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
telescopic\+Value\+Dates optimizes the schedule for calculation speed, but might fail to produce correct results if the coupon ages by more than a grace period of 7 days. It is therefore recommended not to set this flag to true unless you know exactly what you are doing. The intended use is rather by the \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_o_i_s_rate_helper}{OISRate\+Helper} which is safe, since it reinitialises the instrument each time the evaluation date changes. 
\end{DoxyWarning}
