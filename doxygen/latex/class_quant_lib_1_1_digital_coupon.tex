\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Digital-\/payoff coupon. 

Implementation of a floating-\/rate coupon with digital call/put option. Payoffs\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_coupon}{Coupon} with cash-\/or-\/nothing Digital Call rate + csi \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} payoff\+Rate \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} Heaviside(rate-\/strike)
\item \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_coupon}{Coupon} with cash-\/or-\/nothing Digital Put rate + csi \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} payoff\+Rate \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} Heaviside(strike-\/rate) where csi=+1 or csi=-\/1.
\item \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_coupon}{Coupon} with asset-\/or-\/nothing Digital Call rate + csi \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} rate \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} Heaviside(rate-\/strike)
\item \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_coupon}{Coupon} with asset-\/or-\/nothing Digital Put rate + csi \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} rate \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} Heaviside(strike-\/rate) where csi=+1 or csi=-\/1. If naked\+Option is true, the rate in the payoffs is set to zero. The evaluation of the coupon is made using the call/put spread replication method.
