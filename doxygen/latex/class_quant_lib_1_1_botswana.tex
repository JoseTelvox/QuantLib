\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_botswana}{Botswana} calendar. 

Holidays\+: From the \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_botswana}{Botswana} \href{http://www.ilo.org/dyn/travail/docs/1766/Public\%20Holidays\%20Act.pdf}{\texttt{ Public Holidays Act}} ~\newline
 The days named in the \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_schedule}{Schedule} shall be public holidays within \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_botswana}{Botswana}\+: Provided that 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item when any of the said days fall on a Sunday the following Monday shall be observed as a public holiday; 
\item if 2nd January, 1st October or Boxing \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day} falls on a Monday, the following Tuesday shall be observed as a public holiday; 
\item when \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_botswana}{Botswana} \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day} referred to in the \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_schedule}{Schedule} falls on a Saturday, the next following Monday shall be observed as a public holiday. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
