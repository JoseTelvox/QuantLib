\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_israel}{Israel} calendar. 

Due to the lack of reliable sources, the settlement calendar has the same holidays as the Tel Aviv stock-\/exchange.

Holidays for the Tel-\/\+Aviv \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_stock}{Stock} Exchange (data from \href{http://www.tase.co.il}{\texttt{ http\+://www.\+tase.\+co.\+il}})\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Friday 
\item Saturday 
\end{DoxyItemize}Other holidays for wich no rule is given (data available for 2013-\/2044 only\+:) 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Purim, Adar 14th (between Feb 24th \& Mar 26th) 
\item Passover I, Nisan 15th (between Mar 26th \& Apr 25th) 
\item Passover VII, Nisan 21st (between Apr 1st \& May 1st) 
\item Memorial \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, Nisan 27th (between Apr 7th \& May 7th) 
\item Indipendence \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, Iyar 5th (between Apr 15th \& May 15th) 
\item Pentecost (Shavuot), Sivan 6th (between May 15th \& June 14th) 
\item Fast \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day} 
\item Jewish New \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a5dcda5470aff130981ac30f25d4244e7}{Year}, Tishrei 1st \& 2nd (between Sep 5th \& Oct 5th) 
\item Yom Kippur, Tishrei 10th (between Sep 14th \& Oct 14th) 
\item Sukkoth, Tishrei 15th (between Sep 19th \& Oct 19th) 
\item Simchat Tora, Tishrei 22nd (between Sep 26th \& Oct 26th) 
\end{DoxyItemize}
