Uniform random number generator. 

Random number generator of L\textquotesingle{}Ecuyer with added Bays-\/\+Durham shuffle (know as ran2 in Numerical recipes)

For more details see Section 7.\+1 of Numerical Recipes in C, 2nd Edition, Cambridge University Press (available at \href{http://www.nr.com/}{\texttt{ http\+://www.\+nr.\+com/}})
