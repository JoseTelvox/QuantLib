\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Exponential-\/splines fitting method. 

Fits a discount function to the exponential form   \[d(t) = \sum_{i=1}^9 c_i e^{-\kappa_i t}
\] where the constants $ c_i $ and $ \kappa $ are to be determined. See\+:Li, B., E. De\+Wetering, G. Lucas, R. Brenner and A. Shapiro (2001)\+: "{}\+Merrill Lynch Exponential Spline \+Model."{} Merrill Lynch Working Paper

$ \kappa $ can be passed a fixed value, in which case it is excluded from optimization.

\begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
convergence may be slow 
\end{DoxyWarning}
