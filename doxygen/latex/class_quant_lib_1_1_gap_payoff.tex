\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Binary gap payoff. 

This payoff is equivalent to being a) long a \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_plain_vanilla_payoff}{Plain\+Vanilla\+Payoff} at the first strike (same Call/\+Put type) and b) short a \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_cash_or_nothing_payoff}{Cash\+Or\+Nothing\+Payoff} at the first strike (same Call/\+Put type) with cash payoff equal to the difference between the second and the first strike. \begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
this payoff can be negative depending on the strikes 
\end{DoxyWarning}
