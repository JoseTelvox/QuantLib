\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Drift computation for log-\/normal Libor market models. 

Returns the drift $ \mu \Delta t $. See Mark Joshi, {\itshape Rapid Computation of Drifts in a Reduced Factor \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_libor}{Libor} Market Model}, Wilmott Magazine, May 2003.
