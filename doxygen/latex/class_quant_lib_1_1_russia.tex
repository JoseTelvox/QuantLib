\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Russian calendars. 

Public holidays (see \href{http://www.cbr.ru/eng/}{\texttt{ http\+://www.\+cbr.\+ru/eng/}}\+:)\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Saturdays 
\item Sundays 
\item New \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a5dcda5470aff130981ac30f25d4244e7}{Year} holidays, January 1st to 5th (only 1st and 2nd until 2005) 
\item Christmas, January 7th (possibly moved to Monday) 
\item Defender of the Fatherland \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, February 23rd (possibly moved to Monday) 
\item International Women\textquotesingle{}s \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, March 8th (possibly moved to Monday) 
\item Labour \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, May 1st (possibly moved to Monday) 
\item Victory \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, May 9th (possibly moved to Monday) 
\item \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_russia}{Russia} \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, June 12th (possibly moved to Monday) 
\item Unity \doxylink{namespace_quant_lib_a7354a4ac9189352870cbb0cb998e0bd4}{Day}, November 4th (possibly moved to Monday) 
\end{DoxyItemize}

Holidays for the Moscow Exchange (MOEX) taken from \href{http://moex.com/s726}{\texttt{ http\+://moex.\+com/s726}} and related pages. These holidays are {\itshape not} consistent year-\/to-\/year, may or may not correlate to public holidays, and are only available for dates since the introduction of the MOEX \textquotesingle{}brand\textquotesingle{} (a merger of the stock and futures markets).
