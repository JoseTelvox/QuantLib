\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Synthetic Collateralized Debt Obligation. 

The instrument prices a mezzanine \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_c_d_o}{CDO} tranche with loss given default between attachment point $ D_1$ and detachment point $ D_2 > D_1 $.

For purchased protection, the instrument value is given by the difference of the protection value $ V_1 $ and premium value $ V_2 $,

\[ V = V_1 - V_2. \]

The protection leg is priced as follows\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Build the probability distribution for volume of defaults $ L $ (before recovery) or \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_loss}{Loss} Given \doxylink{struct_quant_lib_1_1_default}{Default} $ LGD = (1-r)\,L $ at times/dates $ t_i, i=1, ..., N$ (premium schedule times with intermediate steps)
\item Determine the expected value $ E_i = E_{t_i}\,\left[Pay(LGD)\right] $ of the protection payoff $ Pay(LGD) $ at each time $ t_i$ where         \[Pay(L) = min (D_1, LGD) - min (D_2, LGD) = \left\{
\begin{array}{lcl}
\displaystyle 0 &;& LGD < D_1 \\
\displaystyle LGD - D_1 &;& D_1 \leq LGD \leq D_2 \\
\displaystyle D_2 - D_1 &;& LGD > D_2
\end{array}
\right.
\]
\item The protection value is then calculated as \[ V_1 \:=\: \sum_{i=1}^N (E_i - E_{i-1}) \cdot  d_i \] where $ d_i$ is the discount factor at time/date $ t_i $
\end{DoxyItemize}

The premium is paid on the protected notional amount, initially $ D_2 - D_1. $ This notional amount is reduced by the expected protection payments $ E_i $ at times $ t_i, $ so that the premium value is calculated as

\[V_2 =m \, \cdot \sum_{i=1}^N \,(D_2 - D_1 - E_i) \cdot \Delta_{i-1,i}\,d_i
\]

where $ m $ is the premium rate, $ \Delta_{i-1, i}$ is the day count fraction between date/time $ t_{i-1}$ and $ t_i.$

The construction of the portfolio loss distribution $ E_i $ is based on the probability bucketing algorithm described in

{\bfseries{ John Hull and Alan White, "{}\+Valuation of a CDO and nth to default CDS without Monte Carlo simulation"{}, Journal of Derivatives 12, 2, 2004 }}

The pricing algorithm allows for varying notional amounts and default termstructures of the underlyings.
