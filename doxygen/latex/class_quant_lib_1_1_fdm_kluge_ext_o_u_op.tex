\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This class describes a correlated Kluge -\/ extended Ornstein-\/\+Uhlenbeck process governed by           \[\begin{array}{rcl}
    P_t &=& \exp(p_t + X_t + Y_t) \\
    dX_t &=& -\alpha X_tdt + \sigma_x dW_t^x \\
    dY_t &=& -\beta Y_{t-}dt + J_tdN_t \\
    \omega(J) &=& \eta e^{-\eta J} \\
    G_t &=& \exp(g_t + U_t) \\
    dU_t &=& -\kappa U_tdt + \sigma_udW_t^u \\
    \rho &=& \mathrm{corr} (dW_t^x, dW_t^u)
 \end{array}
\]

References\+: Kluge, Timo L., 2008. Pricing Swing Options and other Electricity Derivatives, \href{http://eprints.maths.ox.ac.uk/246/1/kluge.pdf}{\texttt{ http\+://eprints.\+maths.\+ox.\+ac.\+uk/246/1/kluge.\+pdf}}
