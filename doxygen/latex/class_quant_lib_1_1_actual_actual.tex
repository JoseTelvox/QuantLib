\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Actual/\+Actual day count. 

The day count can be calculated according to\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item the ISDA convention, also known as "{}\+Actual/\+Actual (\+Historical)"{}, "{}\+Actual/\+Actual"{}, "{}\+Act/\+Act"{}, and according to ISDA also "{}\+Actual/365"{}, "{}\+Act/365"{}, and "{}\+A/365"{};
\item the ISMA and US Treasury convention, also known as "{}\+Actual/\+Actual (\+Bond)"{};
\item the AFB convention, also known as "{}\+Actual/\+Actual (\+Euro)"{}.
\end{DoxyItemize}
