\doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_loss}{Loss} distribution for equal volumes but varying probabilities of default.

The method builds the exact loss distribution for a homogeneous pool of underlyings iteratively by computing the convolution of the given loss distribution with the "{}loss distribution"{} of an additional credit following

Xiaofong Ma, "{}\+Numerical Methods for the Valuation of Synthetic \+Collateralized Debt Obligations"{}, PhD Thesis, Graduate Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto, 2007 ~\newline
 \href{http://www.cs.toronto.edu/pub/reports/na/ma-07-phd.pdf}{\texttt{ http\+://www.\+cs.\+toronto.\+edu/pub/reports/na/ma-\/07-\/phd.\+pdf}} (formula 2.\+1)

avoiding numerical instability of the algorithm by

John Hull and Alan White, "{}\+Valuation of a CDO and nth to default CDS  without Monte Carlo simulation"{}, Journal of Derivatives 12, 2, 2004
