\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_bond}{Bond} Market Association index. 

The BMA index is the short-\/term tax-\/exempt reference index of the \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_bond}{Bond} Market Association. It has tenor one week, is fixed weekly on Wednesdays and is applied with a one-\/day\textquotesingle{}s fixing gap from Thursdays on for one week. It is the tax-\/exempt correspondent of the 1M USD-\/\+Libor.
