\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Simple day counter for reproducing theoretical calculations. 

This day counter tries to ensure that whole-\/month distances are returned as a simple fraction, i.\+e., 1 year = 1.\+0, 6 months = 0.\+5, 3 months = 0.\+25 and so forth.

\begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
this day counter should be used together with \doxylink{class_quant_lib_1_1_null_calendar}{Null\+Calendar}, which ensures that dates at whole-\/month distances share the same day of month. It is {\bfseries{not}} guaranteed to work with any other calendar.
\end{DoxyWarning}
